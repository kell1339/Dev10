Welcome to Kitty Kafe - Fakeville's very first cat cafe! We offer food that is sure to please all eaters. This cafe also includes a kitty room where you can cozy up with a feline friend while you enjoy a cold beverage or delicious meal. Our kitty room houses 10-12 rescue cats from local shelters that are all available for adoption. So as not to stress our cats, space in the kitty room is limited and on a first come first serve basis.
